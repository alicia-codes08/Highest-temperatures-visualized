This visual contains dots that represents the individual measured temperatures in Zurich, while the thin lines 
connects the dots. This visual gives one an overview of the temperatues over the year. 
The measured highest temperatues are in the beginning and the end of the year rather low compared to the 
highest temperatues in the middle of the year.

Origin of the data:
https://www.meteoschweiz.admin.ch/service-und-publikationen/applikationen/ext/daten-ohne-programmierkenntnisse-herunterladen.html
